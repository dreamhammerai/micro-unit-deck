The Housing Opportunity & Investment Thesis

This section outlines the macroeconomic drivers behind our strategy. We aim to capitalize on shifting demographics by creating smaller, more affordable, and highly efficient living spaces. Review the core market drivers and our precise target property profile below.

↗

Major Housing Demand Shift

  • Rising housing costs across U.S. markets
  • Growing renter population seeking affordability
  • Young professionals prioritizing location over space
  • Limited supply of well-designed efficient micro-units

Key Insight

Smaller units inherently generate a significantly higher rent per square foot than standard layouts.

The Core Strategy

Acquire underutilized single-family homes and convert them into multiple self-contained micro-units.

📈 Higher Rental Income
💵 Forced Appreciation (NOI)
🔄 Repeatable Acquisition
🏢 Scalable Portfolio

Target Property Profile

  • ✓ 3–5 bedroom homes (1,500–2,200 sq ft)
  • ✓ Favorable lot layouts for private entrances
  • ✓ Strong rental demand neighborhoods
  • ✓ Purchase price below stabilized multifamily value
